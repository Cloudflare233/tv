## Example 02

Solving the equation: 2x-1=0

Solution: The original equation can be transformed into: 0x^2+2x-1=0

From the root-finding formula: x=(-2±√(4+4×0))/(2×0)=(-1±√(1+0))/0

Obviously: x_1=(-2)/0 is meaningless
x_2=(0+1/2 √(1/(1+0)))/1=1/2

To sum up: the solution of the original equation is x=0.5
